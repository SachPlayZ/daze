# Daze fantasy scoring v1

This is the canonical player-scoring matrix. `packages/scoring/src/index.ts` is the executable source of truth; this document must change in the same review as any scoring-rule change.

## Rule matrix

| Event | GK | DEF | MID | FWD | Provider status | Notes |
| --- | ---: | ---: | ---: | ---: | --- | --- |
| Confirmed starting appearance | +1 | +1 | +1 | +1 | VERIFIED | Once per confirmed starter after match start. |
| Confirmed substitute appearance | +1 | +1 | +1 | +1 | VERIFIED | Once when the player enters. |
| 60 active minutes | +1 | +1 | +1 | +1 | VERIFIED | At cumulative active seconds `>= 3600`. |
| Goal | +6 | +6 | +5 | +4 | VERIFIED | Confirmed non-penalty-shootout goal with resolved scorer. |
| Penalty goal | +6 | +6 | +5 | +4 | VERIFIED | Confirmed regulation/extra-time `penalty_outcome` with resolved taker. |
| Penalty miss | -2 | -2 | -2 | -2 | DISABLED | Requires verified taker/outcome and retake handling. |
| Own goal | -2 | -2 | -2 | -2 | VERIFIED | Confirmed own-goal amendment with one unambiguous superseded goal; ambiguous amendments are quarantined. |
| Yellow card | -1 | -1 | -1 | -1 | VERIFIED | Confirmed `yellow_card` with a resolved recipient; official discard reverses it. |
| Direct red card | -3 | -3 | -3 | -3 | VERIFIED | Confirmed `red_card` with `Type=StraightRed` and a resolved recipient. |
| Second yellow adjustment | -2 | -2 | -2 | -2 | VERIFIED | Added only after the existing yellow, for a total card deduction of -3. |
| Clean sheet | +4 | +4 | +1 | 0 | VERIFIED | Provisional after 60 active minutes with no conceded goals; reverses if broken while active. |
| Goals conceded | -1 | -1 | 0 | 0 | VERIFIED | Active GK/DEF only, at the second, fourth, sixth, etc. conceded goal. |

## Multipliers and reversals

- Captain: `2x` every positive and negative ledger delta, including reversals.
- Vice-captain: stays `1x` live. At final replay only, receives a second copy of finalized base points if the captain recorded exactly zero active seconds.
- Provider correction: reverse the prior ledger row, then append the replacement. Do not mutate history.
- Penalty shootout (`StatusId 12` / `PE`): zero fantasy points, including shootout goals.

## What is intentionally not scored in v1

TxLINE may return shots, corners, possession, passes, tackles, saves, interceptions, key passes, ratings, team stat totals, and other match context. None affects fantasy points in v1.

Team totals for yellow/red cards never award an individual player deduction. Daze requires a confirmed player-level action, a verified player-ID join, duplicate handling, amendment handling, and replay coverage before a gated row becomes `VERIFIED`.

## Release rule

A row marked `SHADOW` may be persisted and compared, but cannot change points, ranks, settlement, or user notifications. A row marked `DISABLED` is ignored except for immutable raw-payload storage.
